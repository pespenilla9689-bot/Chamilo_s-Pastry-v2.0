Layers of story in every slice

From a small home oven to a full-service cake studio, Chamilo's Pastry was baked from a love for celebrations, color, and just the right amount of sugar.

Founder story

It started with one birthday cake

What began as a way to make family birthdays extra special quickly turned into custom orders from friends, then friends-of-friends, and now hundreds of clients every year.

Today, Chamilo's Pastry specializes in bespoke celebration cakes, minimalist buttercream designs, and modern dessert tables for weddings, birthdays, and corporate events.

We believe cakes should taste as incredible as they look. Every sponge, filling, and frosting is made from scratch in small batches using premium ingredients.
